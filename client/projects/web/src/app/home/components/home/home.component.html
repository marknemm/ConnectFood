What is Food Web!

FoodWeb donations and deliveries are suspended until further notice due to the concerns surrounding the COVID-19 outbreak. We apologize for any inconvenience, and look forward to starting back up when safe to do so. Thank you for your support and understanding!

Hundreds of pounds of food are thrown out by local food industries.

There are more than 8,200 homeless people in Western New York.

Over 50% of the youth in the city of Buffalo live below the poverty line.

We are a non-profit whose goal is to facilitate the delivery of food from generous donors to those who need it the most. Through our website and community engagement, we hope to address food insecurity while also providing an avenue for citizens to give back to their community.

Learn More

Our mission

is to help alleviate hunger and prevent the waste of food. With our platform and our network of volunteers, we will enable local businesses to easily and quickly donate their excess food to local charities.

Sandwich Drive
On: April 17th 2021

FoodWeb's weekly sandwich drives to benefit St. Luke's Mission for Mercy continue on April 17. Please join FoodWeb in making sandwich lunches consisting of a sandwich (turkey, ham, bologna preferred), a piece of fruit, and a snack (cookie or chips) and putting it all in a Ziploc or lunch bag. PLEASE LABEL the sandwiches so our volunteers can identify sandwiches containing perishable items. You can drop off the sandwich lunches at any of the below drop-off centers between 1:30 pm – 2:30 pm on April 17(please note changed drop-off window). Our volunteers will deliver to St. Luke’s.
