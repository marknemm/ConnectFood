Donation Hubs are designated locations for sandwich-makers to drop off their bagged lunches between 1:30-2:30 pm on the date of the sandwich drive. Volunteers from the hubs must transport the sandwiches to St. Luke’s Mission for Mercy before 3:30 pm.

Drivers will go to the Good Shepherd House. It is located by turning left after entering St. Luke’s parking lot, turning left through a side road, and finally taking a quick left. Drop off the sandwiches on the porch, and then call Gino at (716) 830-1413 to let him know to pick them up.

SPECIAL: Christmas Eve Schedule
  • Pledgers: Drop off your donations between 10:30-11:30 am.
  • Hub Owners: Deliver all donation drop-offs to St. Luke's at 12:30 pm.
