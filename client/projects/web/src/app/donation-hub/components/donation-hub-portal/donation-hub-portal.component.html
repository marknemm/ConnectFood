device_hub Drop-Off Hub

Every Saturday, we rely on volunteers to serve as donation drop-off hubs within the Sandwich Army.

You can register your home as a drop-off hub, and other volunteers will pledge donations to it.

You can easily monitor expected donation drop-offs & edit or remove your Donation Drop-Off Hubs.

You will be sent reminder emails 24 hours and 1 hour before your hub's drop-off window.

After the drop-off window ends, you are expected to transport all sandwiches to the Good Shepherd House, which is located at St. Luke's Mission for Mercy.

After delivering the sandwiches, you should notify Gino about the delivery at (716) 830-1413.

Donation Pledge

You can pledge donations to our Donation Drop-Off Hubs, which should be available each Saturday.

You can easily view & cancel your Donation Pledges whenever you wish.

You are expected to prepare and package all sandwiches before the drop-off window for your chosen hub.

You will be sent reminder emails 24 hours and 1 hour before your donation's drop-off window.

Please follow any instructions provided by the owner of the donation drop-off hub.

Feel free to contact the drop-off hub if you have any questions. The hub's contact information can be seen when viewing your Donation Pledges.
