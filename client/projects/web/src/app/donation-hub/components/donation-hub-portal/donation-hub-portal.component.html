Sandwich Army

The Sandwich Army's mission is to provide a consistent, reliable source of food for the homeless. Every Saturday volunteers donate sandwiches to volunteer run drop-off hubs between 1:30 PM - 2:30 PM. After the drop-off time, the hubs deliver the sandwiches to St. Luke's Mission of Mercy.

Out goal is to provide at least 100 sandwiches every Saturday. If you are interested in volunteering, please read the information below.

device_hub Register Drop-Off Hub

Register your address as a Drop-Off Hub for an upcoming Saturday.

You are expected to do the following:

  • Collect lunches delivered by other volunteers between 1:30 PM - 2:30 PM.
  • Deliver all lunches to St. Luke's between 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM.

Additionally, feel free to make sandwiches and donate them to your own hub.

We prefer that volunteers register as drop-off hubs if they can commit to driving their sandwiches directly to St. Luke's.

Donate Sandwiches

Donate bagged sandwich lunches to our Drop-Off Hubs each Saturday.

You are expected to do the following:

  • Make a minimum of 20 sandwiches of your choice.
  • Wrap each sandwich in either aluminum foil or plastic wrap.
  • Supply a small snack and piece of fruit with each sandwich.
  • Combine the wrapped sandwich, snack, and piece of fruit in a paper bag.

Please prepare all sandwiches in a sanitary environment, and keep all perishable items refrigerated until drop-off. Also, we prefer that you avoid the heavy use of condiments and bananas as your choice of fruit.
