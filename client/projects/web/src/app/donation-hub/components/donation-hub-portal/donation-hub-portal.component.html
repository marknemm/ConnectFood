device_hub Donation Drop-Off Hub

You can register your home as a drop-off hub for an upcoming Saturday, and other volunteers will pledge donations to it.

After the drop-off window ends, you are expected to deliver all sandwiches to the Good Shepherd House, which is located at St. Luke's Mission for Mercy.

After delivery, you should notify Gino about the drop-off at (716) 830-1413.

Donation Pledge

You can donate to our Donation Drop-Off Hubs, which should be available each Saturday.

You are expected to prepare and package a minimum of 20 sandwiches before the drop-off window for your chosen hub.

Please also try to include a snack such as a piece of fruit with each sandwich.
