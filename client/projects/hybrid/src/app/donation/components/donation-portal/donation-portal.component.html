Donate

Donate using our donation form, and our platform will automatically match your gift with a local charity.

Once your donation is matched with a receiving charity, our system will notify our dedicated network of volunteer drivers to perform the delivery.

You have full control over the time window during which a volunteer may pickup your donation. Feel free to schedule a donation at any time.

Never hesitate to review, modify, or delete Your Donations in order to accommodate your schedule.

On behalf of all of our neighbors who benefit from your gift, we thank you for your generosity!
