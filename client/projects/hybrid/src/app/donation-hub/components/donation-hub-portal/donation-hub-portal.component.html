Donation Drop-Off Hub

You can register your home as a drop-off hub for an upcoming Saturday, and other volunteers will pledge donations to it.

After the drop-off window ends, you are expected to deliver all sandwiches to the Norman Paolini Jr House of Recovery (Good Shepherd Residence), which is located at St. Luke's Mission for Mercy.

Donation Pledge

You can donate to our Donation Drop-Off Hubs, which should be available each Saturday.

You are expected to prepare and package a minimum of 20 sandwiches before the drop-off window for your chosen hub.

Please also try to include a snack such as a piece of fruit with each sandwich.

Donation Hub

Every Saturday, we rely on volunteers to serve as Donation Hubs within the Sandwich Army.

Donation hubs serve as intermediate drop-off points within one's neighborhood for food donations during a specified drop-off window.

After the drop-off window ends, you are expected to transport all sandwiches to a designated local charity.

Login or Signup as a Volunteer to register a donation hub or pledge a donation.
