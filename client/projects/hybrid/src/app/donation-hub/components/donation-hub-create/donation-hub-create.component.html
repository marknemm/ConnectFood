Donation Hubs are designated locations for sandwich-makers to drop off their bagged lunches between 1:30-2:30 pm on the date of the sandwich drive. Volunteers from the hubs must transport the sandwiches to St. Luke’s Mission for Mercy before 3:30 pm.

Drivers will go to the Norman Paolini Jr House of Recovery (Good Shepherd Residence). It is located by turning left after entering St. Luke’s parking lot, turning left through a side road, and finally taking a quick left. Ring the bell to notify volunteers that there is a sandwich drop-off.
